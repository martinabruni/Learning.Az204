# Scale Apps in App Service – Introduction

> [!source] learn.microsoft.com_en-us_training_modules_scale-apps-app-service_1-introduction.pdf

## Overview

This module covers [[autoscale|autoscaling]] in [[azure-app-service]] — dynamically adjusting resources to meet demand while managing costs.

> [!source] learn.microsoft.com_en-us_training_modules_scale-apps-app-service_1-introduction.pdf
> "Autoscaling enables a system to adjust the resources required to meet the varying demand from users, while controlling the costs associated with these resources."

## Learning Objectives

- Identify scenarios where autoscaling is appropriate.
- Create autoscale rules for a web app.
- Monitor the effects of autoscaling.
